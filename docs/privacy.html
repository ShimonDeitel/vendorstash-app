Privacy Policy

Vendorstash is an offline-first app. All data you enter (entries, amounts, dates, and notes) is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no analytics SDKs, no ad networks, and no network calls in this app beyond Apple's own App Store / StoreKit purchase verification, which is handled entirely by Apple.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is deleted with it. We have no server, so we have nothing to give you, delete for you, or breach — your data never leaves your device.
